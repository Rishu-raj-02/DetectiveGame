# 🕵️ GSK PROJECT: The Sacramento Cold Case

[![Framework: Next.js](https://img.shields.io/badge/Framework-Next.js-black?style=for-the-badge&logo=next.js)](https://nextjs.org/)
[![Style: Cinematic_Noir](https://img.shields.io/badge/Style-Cinematic_Noir-bc2f32?style=for-the-badge)](https://nextjs.org/)

> **"The archives hold the secrets. You hold the reasoning."**

GSK PROJECT is a high-fidelity, noir-inspired detective simulation. Step into a digital crime lab, analyze authentic forensic evidence from the Golden State Killer case, and piece together the profile of one of history's most elusive offenders.

![Main Banner](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1505664194779-8beaceb93744?q=80&w=2000&auto=format&fit=crop)

---

## 📽️ The Cinematic Experience

- **📺 Terminal Interface**: A bespoke CRT scanline engine with procedural film grain and flickering terminal effects.
- **📁 Authentic Archiving**: High-resolution forensic assets including DNA charting, tactical maps, and archival crime scene logs.
- **🎵 Atmospheric Soundscape**: A custom audio engine delivering a high-stakes, evolving detective score.
- **⏪ Investigative Rigor**: Dynamic stage regression allows you to revisit past evidence and pivot your theories as new links emerge.
